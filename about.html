About Us

Bommireddy C Nishant
Founder

Hey, I'm Nishant, a student navigating the exciting journey of freshman year while also diving into the whirlwind world of toddler parenting. Juggling textbooks and tantrums might seem like an odd combo, but it's my reality. Parenthood hit me early, and I quickly realized the need for a guide that speaks to fellow students tackling the challenges of raising toddlers. This website is a blend of my personal experiences, the wisdom I've gained, and a dash of humor to make the journey a bit more manageable. Join me in this adventure where I share insights, tips, and a sense of camaraderie for parents who, like me, are balancing the demands of academia and parenting. Let's navigate this rollercoaster together because, after all, we're in the same boat!
